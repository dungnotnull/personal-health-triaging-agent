## Update Protocol

1. Crawler fetches new published guidelines from PubMed, WHO, CDC, Vietnam MOH
2. `clinical_summarizer.py` extracts actionable clinical rules
3. Proposed changes are appended to the appropriate section below with `[PROPOSED]` prefix
4. Clinical advisor reviews all `[PROPOSED]` entries before they are promoted to `[APPROVED]`
5. Only `[APPROVED]` entries may be used to update `red_flags.yaml` or `triage_rules.yaml`
