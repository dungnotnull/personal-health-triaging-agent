# Medical Disclaimer — Personal Health Triaging Agent (PHTA)

**Effective Date:** 2025-05-31  
**Applies to:** All versions of PHTA, all output channels (text, voice, web UI, API)

---

## 1. Not a Medical Device

PHTA is a **consumer health information tool** that provides triage screening recommendations based on the symptoms and data you provide. It is **not** a medical device, diagnostic tool, or substitute for professional medical advice.

PHTA does **not**:
- Diagnose diseases or conditions
- Prescribe medications or treatments
- Replace consultation with a licensed physician, nurse, or other qualified healthcare professional

---

## 2. Informational Purpose Only

All output from PHTA — including triage levels, symptom interpretations, wearable data interpretations, nutrition guidance, and monitoring plans — is provided for **informational purposes only**.

The triage level recommendations (EMERGENCY / URGENT / SEMI-URGENT / NON-URGENT) are:
- Generated by a combination of rule-based algorithms and machine learning models
- Based on patterns in the symptom descriptions and wearable data you provide
- Designed to help you make more informed decisions about seeking care
- **Not** a substitute for clinical judgment

---

## 3. Emergency Warning

**If PHTA outputs an EMERGENCY (RED) triage level, this means the system has detected patterns consistent with potentially life-threatening conditions. You should:**

1. Call emergency services immediately (Vietnam: **115**)
2. If you are experiencing chest pain, difficulty breathing, signs of stroke, severe allergic reaction, active suicidal thoughts, or loss of consciousness — **DO NOT WAIT** — seek emergency care immediately, regardless of what PHTA outputs
3. PHTA may fail to detect an emergency. **Trust your instincts.** If you feel something is seriously wrong, seek emergency care.

---

## 4. Mental Health Crisis

If you are experiencing thoughts of self-harm or suicide:
- Call the Vietnam Mental Health Crisis Hotline: **1800 599 920** (free, 24/7)
- Go to the nearest hospital emergency department
- Contact a trusted person immediately

PHTA includes a mental health crisis detection protocol, but it may not detect all expressions of psychological distress. If you are in crisis, please use the resources above regardless of what PHTA outputs.

---

## 5. Limitations

PHTA has **inherent limitations**:

- **Incomplete information:** PHTA only processes the symptoms you describe and the wearable data you provide. It cannot perform a physical examination, review your full medical history, or order diagnostic tests.
- **Algorithmic errors:** The system may under-triage (fail to identify a serious condition) or over-triage (recommend urgent care for a benign condition). When in doubt, it errs toward the more urgent recommendation.
- **Language limitations:** Vietnamese and English are supported. Medical terminology in other languages may not be processed accurately.
- **Wearable data accuracy:** Consumer wearable devices may provide inaccurate readings. PHTA treats wearable data as supporting evidence, not definitive clinical measurements.
- **Rare conditions:** The system is trained on common presentations. Rare diseases or atypical presentations may not be recognized.

---

## 6. No Doctor-Patient Relationship

Use of PHTA does **not** establish a doctor-patient relationship with any individual or organization. PHTA is software, not a healthcare provider.

---

## 7. Data Privacy

PHTA is designed for **local-first operation**. By default, your health data remains on your device. See the Privacy Policy for details on data handling, encryption, and your rights.

Under Vietnam's Personal Data Protection Decree 13/2023/NĐ-CP, you have the right to access, correct, and delete your personal data at any time by using the `phta delete-my-data` command.

---

By using PHTA, you acknowledge that:
- You have read and understood this disclaimer
- You understand that PHTA is not a substitute for professional medical advice
- You agree to consult a qualified healthcare professional for any medical concerns
- You accept all risks associated with the use of this software

**This is not a medical diagnosis. Always consult a qualified healthcare professional.**
